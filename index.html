Frequently Asked Questions

You asked, we're answering. Top questions about QR Code Reader.

What is a QR Code?

QR Code stands for Quick Response Code and is barcode type that was invented by Denso Wave in 1994. Get more information at Wikipedia.

Is there a scan limit for the QR Codes?

There are no limits. Scan it as many times as you wish!

Can I use the generated QR Codes for commercial purposes?

Yes, all QR Codes generated with our application are free and can be used for just about anything.

Is QR Code Reader saving my data?

Your data is yours, saved locally on the device only. We do not collect or store any bit of information. So we can't use it, can't share it, and can't sell it.

How can I support QR Code Reader?

Glad you asked! To keep QR Code Reader free we need your support! There are several things you can do for us: Recommendations, links to QR Code Reader, or even a blog entry about our app can help us a lot.
